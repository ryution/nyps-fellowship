The Academy Fellowship — Summer 2026

Applications for the Summer 2026 cohort are now open. The program runs from July 13 through August 23, 2026 in Midtown Manhattan. The Fellowship is fully funded — no tuition, no application fee, and need-based stipends are available to cover materials and transportation.

Applications Now Open

Applications close May 15, 2026. The application consists of two short essays, an official transcript, and a brief interview. There is no application fee.

Format & Location

Fellows meet one to two times per week in Midtown Manhattan for extended seminars of two to three hours. Between sessions, fellows complete 10–15 hours of independent reading. The program is a commuter program — no residential component.

For questions, contact Harrison Orlando at fellowship@nyphilosophy.org.
